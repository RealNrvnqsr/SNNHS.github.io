ABOUT STO NIÑO NATIONAL HIGHSCHOOL

School Overview

Sto Niño National Highschool is an educational institution that aims to shape the minds of our future nation builders. The school is committed to providing a safe and inclusive environment where students are encouraged to explore their potential, think critically, and contribute positively to their community.

Images taken by The Saligan and Ang Saligan. All rights belong to the respective owners.

Sto Niño National Highschool offers various extracurricular activities that help enhance students’ talents, leadership skills, and personal growth beyond the classroom. These activities provide opportunities for learners to explore their interests, build confidence, and develop teamwork and communication skills while fostering school spirit and camaraderie.

In addition, the school also provides strand-specific activities and programs designed to strengthen students’ practical knowledge and real-world experience. These activities allow learners to apply what they have studied in class, further develop their technical and creative skills, and prepare them for future academic and career opportunities.

Senior Highschool Strands Info

Sto Niño National Highschool provides two main Senior High School strands: ICT (Computer Programming) and Home Economics (Cookery).

Each strand has specialized facilities designed to support their specific learning needs.

ICT (Computer Programming)

Computer Programming students may use the Computer Laboratories to apply and enhance their programming skills. Our ICT learning program focuses on developing technical knowledge, problem-solving abilities, and real-world programming experience.

Learn more about the ICT (Computer Programming) Strand

Home Economics (Cookery)

Home Economics students may use the Kitchen Laboratories to practice and apply various cooking techniques while developing culinary skills and proper kitchen management.
